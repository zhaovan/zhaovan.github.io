About

Hi there, I'm Ivan!

I'm an entrepreneur, designer, and engineer based in Bellevue, WA. I'm currently working for Loftium, a Seattle-based startup creating a market for short-term leases. Previously, I also worked on a small project called Scope that connected high school seniors with a college student at their respective college and major of interest and have also completed a research project about the startup ecosystem in the Greater Seattle Area.

My academic interests range from optimizing space, both physical and online, by utilizing design properties and thinking to applications of game theory in both actual economic markets and board games. I'm generally interested all facets of life and will talk and listen to anything, ranging from the architecture of the Chiesa di San Carlino alle Quattro Fontane to the amazing symbolism in the game Celeste, an Indie game that won best Indie Game and best Game for Impact in 2018 (I highly recommend checking it out). 

Other things of interest: human-computer interaction and how we use technology to define the world around us, art history, the notion of design and how it affects our day-to-day lifestyle, LGBTQ+ support in the startup and venture capital scene around the world, board games, baking, rock climbing, and music/musicals.

If any of those things sound interesting, feel free to reach out to me! I'd also love to talk about project ideas or anything else :)

Projects

Ply: Visual Web Inspection

Delta Lab

research development

CSS is syntactically straightforward, but has a steep learning curve and complicated semantics. Inspecting the source of existing webpages can help illustrate concepts, but such webpages are typically too complex to serve as useful learning materials. Drawing inspiration from prior research in both software engineering and the learning sciences, we present a new web inspection tool and set of techniques for pruning irrelevant CSS and identifying implicit dependencies between properties. Supervised by Haoqi Zhang and Nell O’Rourke. Honorable Mention Paper at UIST 2018, Berlin.
