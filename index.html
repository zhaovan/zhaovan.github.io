About

Hi there, I'm Ivan!

I'm currently a Freshman at Brown studying Applied Math - Computer Science. I currently work at Nlyst, a Providence-based startup trying to redefine the hiring scene for companies by creating an analyst program for outgoing juniors and seniors. This past summer I also worked on a small project called Scope that connected high school seniors with a college student at their respective college and major of interest.

I'm generally interested in life in general and will talk and listen to anything, ranging from the architecture of the Chiesa di San Carlino alle Quattro Fontane to the amazing amount symbolism in the game Celeste (which I highly recommend checking out). 
Other things of interest: crosswords, art, travelling, doggos, board games, baking and eating Chinese food, entrepreneurship, design, rock climbing, musicals, Arduino, and LGBTQ+ support.
