About

Hi there, I'm Ivan!

I'm an entrepreneur, designer, and engineer based in Bellevue, WA. I'm currently working for Loftium, a Seattle-based startup creating a market for short-term leases. Previously, I also worked on a small project called Scope that connected high school seniors with a college student at their respective college and major of interest and have also completed a research project about the startup ecosystem in the Greater Seattle Area.

My academic interests range from optimizing space, both physical and online, by utilizing design properties and thinking to applications of game theory in both actual economic markets and board games.
