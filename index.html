About

Hi there, I'm Ivan!

I'm currently a Freshman at Brown studying Applied Math and Computer Science. This semester, I'm working as a Business Development Intern at Nlyst, a Providence-based startup trying to revolutionize the entry-level hiring scene for companies by creating an analyst program for outgoing juniors and seniors. This past summer I also worked on a small project called Scope that connected high school seniors with a college student at their respective college and major of interest.

My academic interests range from optimizing space, both physical and online, by utilizing design properties and thinking to applications of game theory in both actual economic markets and board games. I'm generally interested all facets of life and will talk and listen to anything, ranging from the architecture of the  Chiesa di San Carlino alle Quattro Fontane to the amazing symbolism in the game Celeste, an Indie game that won best Indie Game and best Game for Impact in 2018 (I highly recommend checking it out). 

Other things of interest: the intersection of technology, humans, and optimization of operations, art history, entrepreneurship and venture capitalism, interior design and how it affects our living space, LGBTQ+ support in the startup and venture capital scene around the world, traveling, board games, baking and eating Chinese food, rock climbing, and music/musicals.
