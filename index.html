About

Hi there, I'm Ivan!

I'm an entrepreneur, designer, and engineer from Bellevue, WA. I'm currently an undergrad at Brown University and just finished an internship at Loftium, a Seattle based startup disrupting the real-estate industry by creating a market for short-term leases with Airbnbs. I'm currently working on various startup projects ranging from e-commerce gifting to in-house care and have previously completed a research project about the startup ecosystem in the Greater Seattle Area.

My academic interests include figuring out ways to optimize space, both in the physical and digital world, to increase efficiency and create a community in our daily lives. Specifically, I'm interested in the ways we utilize space to create something, whether it's a market, a product, or a place to relax and enjoy the company of friends. I'm also interested in systems, particularly systems thinking, and how companies and individuals are revolutionizing these systems to build interesting new things (check out Notion!). One cool concept that stems from systems thinking is the idea of the network effect.

Other things of interest (in no particular order): human-computer interaction and how we use technology to define the world around us, design thinking in regards to our workspace, home, and technology, LGBTQ+ support in the startup and venture capital scene around the world, Celeste, social deduction games, baking, crosswords, art history, rock climbing, and musicals.

If any of those things sound interesting, feel free to reach out to me! I'd also love to talk about project ideas or anything else :)

Data was analyzed using a multi-variate regression model. Worked under Rob Prufer.
